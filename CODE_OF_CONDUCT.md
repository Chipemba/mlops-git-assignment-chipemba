# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Showing empathy toward other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated fairly.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and repository documentation.
